Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

ShazamMedia ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe in privacy by design. To that end:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or share your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No User Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the App.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising identifiers to track your activity across other apps or websites.

2. Data Usage and Processing

The primary function of ShazamMedia is to identify media (such as anime) from images you provide. Here is how your data is handled during this process:

  • Image Processing: When you submit an image or video frame for identification, that specific image data is transmitted securely to third-party identification services (such as public anime identification APIs) solely for the purpose of generating search results.
  • Ephemeral Processing: These images are processed transiently to provide the service and are not used by us to identify or track individual users.

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All of your search history, results, watchlists, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not transmit or backup your personal search history to any servers owned or controlled by ShazamMedia. If you delete the App, this data is removed from your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may interact with third-party APIs to provide identification results. These third parties act as independent data controllers or processors. While we do not control their policies, we select services that align with our functionality needs. We do not share your personal identity with these services.
